Flat iron steak is cut from beef shoulder. To make a flat iron steak, butchers remove the connective tissue to separate the top shoulder blade into two cuts: One is the top blade, the other is the flat iron.

Ingredients

  • Beef
  • Garlic
  • Beef tallow
  • Butter
  • Salt & Pepper

Steps

  1. Put the meat in the oven until inside temp reach 42c.
  2. Put tallow into pan make sure it hot before put your meat in.
  3. Once the steak reach 42c, remove from the oven, sear in the pan, flip as much as you want
  4. Put in butter and continuously spoon butter over your steak.
  5. Steak is medium rare when internal temp reach 57c
  6. Rest your steak for 5 min. Enjoy!!
